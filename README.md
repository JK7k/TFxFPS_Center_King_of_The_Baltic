# ThunderFlashxFPS_Center_King_of_The_Baltic

After a brief hiatus, the ThunderFlash organization is returning to active operation and, in collaboration with its key partner FPS Center is proud to present a new tournament dedicated to the Baltic Counter-Strike scene.

**Formal Requirements and Participants**
Teams representing the following countries are eligible to participate in the tournament: Poland, Lithuania, Latvia, and Estonia. A mandatory requirement is to have a minimum of three players in the active roster who hold citizenship of the aforementioned countries.

**Qualifier Stage**
There will be one open qualifying round, hosted via FACEIT platform servers.

The qualifiers are scheduled for June 27–28, 2026.

The entry fee for the open qualifiers is 500 PLN (equivalent to approx. 140 USD / 120 EUR).

**Main and Group Stage**
The main stage of the tournament will be played on the organizer's private servers, secured by AKROS AntiCheat software.

The group stage will take place from July 11 to 18, 2026.

Teams will be divided into 8 groups of 4 teams each.

Group stage matches will be played in a round-robin format, with all competition within a single group concluding on a single match day.

Matches in this stage are scheduled for 18:00, 19:15, and 20:30, and will be played in a Best of One (Bo1) format.

**Playoffs**
Only the best team from each group will advance to the Playoff stage.

The playoffs will be played in a Double Elimination bracket.

All matches in this stage will be played in a Best of Three (Bo3) format.

**LAN Finals**
The top four teams, the upper bracket finalists and the lower bracket finalists will play their decisive matches during a live event (LAN), which will take place on August 8, 2026, at the FPS Center in Warsaw.

**Prize Pool**
The total prize pool is 16,000 PLN (~ 4,400 USD / 3,760 EUR).

It will be distributed as follows:

1st Place: 7,000 PLN (~ 1,920 USD / 1,646 EUR)

2nd Place: 4,500 PLN (~ 1,240 USD / 1,060 EUR)

3rd/4th Place: 2,250 PLN (~ 625 USD / 530 EUR)
